About DMC


Note: This is a quick rundown of what the Devil May Cry series about, details of each game will be coming in the later sections.

The Devil May Cry game series is a hack-n-slash game series developed and published by Capcom. It follows the story of the Legendary Dark Knight Dante, a half demon-human hybrid who becomes the ultimate demon hunter.

The game series began back in 2001, after a failed attempt in creating a new Resident Evil game. It was designed to be the ultimate hack-n-slash experince giving the player an action oriented gameplay system in which they attempt to produce over the top combos. These combos are generated with the weapons and abilties learned throughout the game, offering nearly endless possiblities of unique attack patterns. In order to give the player a challenge a ranking system was created to gauge the players called the "stylish-system". This system ranks the players combos based off how often a player switches up their attack patterns, rewarding those who keep their combos different, rather than simple. The system also encoruages players to avoid enemy attacks, as getting hit resets this raning system resulting in a poor grade at the end of a given mission. This system has become a staple throughout all the games in the series, giving the players the challenge of mastering the combat to become worthy of being the ultimate demon hunter.

Currently there are 5 major Devil May Cry games in the series, along with a few spin-offs.

  • Devil May Cry - the entry in the series released in 2001 on the PS2 game console system
  • Devil May Cry 2 - a sequel to continue the story of Dante's journey released in 2003 also on the PS2 system [MOST FORGETTABLE DMC GAME - trust me on this....]
  • Devil May Cry 3: Dante's Awakening - considered to be the best Devil May Cry game to date, this game introduces Dante's early days of being a demon hunter. It was released in 2005 on the PS2
  • Devil May Cry 4 - follows the story of a new devil hunter called Nero. It released in 2008 on the Xbox 360 and PS3 [It was given a Special Edition in 2015 providing more content to the game]
  • Devil May Cry HD: Collection - a HD remaster of the first 3 DMC games. It was released on the PS3 and Xbox 360 in 2012, and re-released on PS4, Xbox One and PC in 2018
  • Devil May Cry 5 - the latest installment in the series released in 2019 on PS4, Xbox One and PC. [It was given a Special Edition in 2020 for PS5 and Xbox Series X]
  • Devil May Cry: Pinnacle of Combat - a unique Chinese DMC experience to the mobile market [Currently in development by YunchangGame after being given permission by Capcom to create]

The order of release of the DMC games aren't in chronolgical order, to correct time-line based in the games are: 3 -> 1 -> 4 -> 2 -> 5

Devil May Cry 1


The Story

WARNING: SPOILERS PRESENT IN PLOT - LEAVE NOW IF YOU DON'T WANT TO RUIN THE SERIES FOR YOURSELF

The story begins with Dante in his office being attacked by a mysterious women named Trish. To Dantes surpise she just so happens to resemble his mothers appearence, asking him for his aid. She tells Dante that the demon emperor Mundus, the same demon responsible for the death of his mother is planning on returning and must be stopped at all costs. Dante agrees to help her and journeys to Mallet Island to find a mysterious castle gaurded by a variety of demons.

Dante now must fight his way through this demonic army, facing off against different gaurdians such as Phantom, a giant spider like creature who constantly persues him throughout the castle. Upon Phantoms defeat, he comes to realize that Dante is the son of Sparda the legardary knight that has once defeated Mundus himself. This sets Dante off to defeat the other powerful demons lurking in the castle such as Nightmare, and entity that is fear itself, and Griffon a large avian creature. However, one demon he faced was different which was Nelo Angelo the demonic knight. Dante struggled fighting him off and upon nearly being bested by him Nelo Angelo flees at the sight of Dantes amulet. It is later revealed that this demons true identity was Vergil, Dante's twin brother who was now under the control of Mundus. After being forced to fight his own brother, Dante defeats him and is heartbroken he had to put his own family member down. He swears to defeat Mundus at all costs to avenge him.

Nearing Mundus's lair Dante runs into Trish who betrays him, and tells him how she was created by Mundus to lore him to the castle so he could be killed by Mundus. Shocked, and angered Dante overpowers but spares her life. He then continues his persuit to destroying Mundus, who is far more power than he though. After engaging in battle Dante is nearly struck down but is saved by Trish who sacerfices herself to save him. Saddened by this Dante's true demonic power takes over transforming him into a demon resembling his father and chases after Mundus.

After an epic battle Mundus is defeated, and Dante begins to leave the island just to find out Mundus is still alive and must engage in battle once again. Trish comes to the rescue as she had survived survived the blow, and together with Dante seals away Mundus once again. Mundus swears he'll one day return, and get is revenge. Dante and Trish leave the island together, and the two team up and become a devil hunting duo.

There is far more to the story - many details were cut out :)

Devil May Cry 2


The Story

WARNING: SPOILERS PRESENT IN PLOT - LEAVE NOW IF YOU DON'T WANT TO RUIN THE SERIES FOR YOURSELF

The story begins with Dante and a women of the name Lucia meeting in a museum which contains an artifact called the Arcana Medaglia, as it's a key element into releasing the powerful demon Argosax. Lucia asks Dante to accompany her to Vile de Marli where he meets her adoptive mom Matier a women who once fought alongside Sparda. She tells Dante that Arius a evil buiness man plans to summon Argosax, and asks him to stop him. In this game Dante uses a coin flip to determine his actions, and upon landing on heads he agrees.

Dante fights his way to confront Arius, but Lucia arrives there before him and learns she's his creation. She attempts to fight him but fails, to later be saved by Dante when he trades the Arcana to Arius. Lucia grows concerned about Arius now having the artifact and goes after him to stop the ritual. Dante stops her, and say's he'll take care of it and engages in a battle with Arius defeats him. He is then confronted by Lucia who requests Dante to kill her as she fears she'll become a mindless demon to which Dante refuses. A portal then opens up to the demon world where Argosax dwells, causing Dante and Lucia to argue who would enter it to defeat him. Dante leaves it to a coin flip and "wins" it and leaves the coin as a token to remember him to Lucia, entering to portal to confront the powerful demon.

Arius was still alive, and Lucia desides to fight him, ultimately winning the battle. In the demon world Dante faces angainst Agrosax and wins with ease, and discovers the portal was closed, and he'd be trapped in the demon world. He decides to ride his motorcycle deeper into the demon world ending the main story. Back in the human world Lucia discovers the coin Dante gave her was a two faced coin, and decides to go to Dantes office in hope she'll find him there. Sadly he wasn't but she does hear the distant engine of a motorcycle and goes to investigate.

DMC2 is viewed as the worst DMC game in the series, for it's mess of a story. It's best to forget it, and just know Dante is stuck in the demon world at the end.

Devil May Cry 3: Dantes Awakening


The Story

WARNING: SPOILERS PRESENT IN PLOT - LEAVE NOW IF YOU DON'T WANT TO RUIN THE SERIES FOR YOURSELF

This game is a prequel to the series, about Dante's early days of being a demon hunter. It start's off with Dante in his newly yet-to-be-named shop, where a mysterious man enters. This man Dante that he's working with his twin brother Vergil, and speaks about the history of who Sparda was. Before leaving the man says that he was sent to invite Dante by Vergil before vanishing. Dante is then ambushed by a group of demons which he easily defeats. Dante see's a tower emerge from the ground called Temen-ni-gru, which is where his brother is waiting for him. Therefore Dante gears up, and sets off the meet his brother.

When entering the tower, he's greeted by a demonic three headed dog named Cerberus who guards the main entance, telling Dante to leave. Dante decides to fight this demonic foe, and wins with easy obtaining its soul to create a devil arm (demonic weapons Dante creates from the essence of the demons he beats). Before going further a women arrives on a motercycle carring several weapon of her own, peaking Dantes interest, but she remains silent and rides past him. Waiting atop of the tower Vergil is greeted by Arkham (the mysterious man who invited Dante to come to the tower), who informs him of Dantes arrival as well as the women. It's revealed that the women was his very own daughter (Mary) who was out to kill him as he had sacerficed her mother (his wife) to gain power. Vergil wasn't bothered, and only had his interest on facing Dante to obtain the ambulet he carried. This ambulet was broken into two halves that enabled for the power of Sparda to be used, something Vergil seeked (MORE POWER).

Dante battles his way throughout the tower facing many demonic foes and obtaining more demonic weapons along the way. He runs into a strange Jester like being that tells him about the tower, and vanishes before Dante could fight him. Dante proceeds to make his way up the tower finally coming face to face with his twin brother. Vergil explains to Dante he wants the ambulet which Dante refuses to give. The two brothers fight and Dante is overpowered and impaled by his own sword. Dante in anger and having his own blood touch his sword awakes his true demonic power before collapsing in defeat. He then awakes feeling powerful, and proceeds to dive off the tower before being swallowed by a large creature named Leviathan. He fights the monster from within before killing it and running into Mary the women he saw earlier. The two of them split ways and he gives her the nick-name Lady.

Dante proceeds to look for Vergil who was preparing to create the Force Edge they key to obtaining the power of Sparda. Vergil betrays Arkham as he no longer needed him killing him, and proceeds his plan to awakening his fathers power. Dante catches up with Vergil and once again the fight, until they are stopped by Lady who believes Vergil is to blame for her fathers actions. Suddenly the Jester appears amoungst them and thanks them for gathering all the materials needed to awaken Spardas power and reveals that he was Arkham all along. He overpowers Dante and Vergil and uses the blood of his daugter to complete the ritual. Arkham is transported to the top of the tower where he seeks to become a demonic god.

Dante decides to make his way up the tower once again to stop Arkham, and comes face to face with Lady who had the same plan. She explains how he was her father and wanted to avenge her mothers death. Her goal is to kill her father, whom Dante assures he can take care of. Before parting ways she gives him her rocket weapon wishing him luck. Dante arrives to the top of the tower to confront Arkham who had mutated into a hidious demon. Vergil arrives and teams up with Dante bringing Arkham demonic form down, causing Arkham to return to his former self. Vergil tells him that he wishes to keep Sparda's power and requests Dante to hand over the Force Edge and ambulet to which Dante refuses. The two brothers battle, leaving Dante to be the victor, Vergil tells Dante he wishes to stay in the demon world, and proceeds to jump deeper into the abyss before Dante could stop him. Dante returns to the human world with his new sword, and runs into Lady who had finshed off her dying father. The two exchange words and she attempts to confort Dante who is sadden by the loss of his brother. She tells him "devil may cry", as Dante hides his tears claiming it's the rain stating that "devils never cry". Dante returns home and names his shop "Devil May Cry".

Back in the demon world a weaken Vergil is confronted by Mundus who he engages in battle with. The outcome isn't revealed thus leading to the events of the first Devil May Cry game.

Devil May Cry 3 is viewed as the best game in the series, it sets up the story of the future games, as well as introducing Dante and Vergil in their early days. It is also the game that sets up the name of the franchise and the awakening of Dantes demonic power.
