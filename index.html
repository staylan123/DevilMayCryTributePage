About DMC


Note: This is a quick rundown of what the Devil May Cry series about, details of each game will be coming in the later sections.

The Devil May Cry game series is a hack-n-slash game series developed and published by Capcom. It follows the story of the Legendary Dark Knight Dante, a half demon-human hybrid who becomes the ultimate demon hunter.

The game series began back in 2001, after a failed attempt in creating a new Resident Evil game. It was designed to be the ultimate hack-n-slash experince giving the player an action oriented gameplay system in which they attempt to produce over the top combos. These combos are generated with the weapons and abilties learned throughout the game, offering nearly endless possiblities of unique attack patterns. In order to give the player a challenge a ranking system was created to gauge the players called the "stylish-system". This system ranks the players combos based off how often a player switches up their attack patterns, rewarding those who keep their combos different, rather than simple. The system also encoruages players to avoid enemy attacks, as getting hit resets this raning system resulting in a poor grade at the end of a given mission. This system has become a staple throughout all the games in the series, giving the players the challenge of mastering the combat to become worthy of being the ultimate demon hunter.

Currently there are 5 major Devil May Cry games in the series, along with a few spin-offs.

  • Devil May Cry - the entry in the series released in 2001 on the PS2 game console system
  • Devil May Cry 2 - a sequel to continue the story of Dante's journey released in 2003 also on the PS2 system [MOST FORGETTABLE DMC GAME - trust me on this....]
  • Devil May Cry 3: Dante's Awakening - considered to be the best Devil May Cry game to date, this game introduces Dante's early days of being a demon hunter. It was released in 2005 on the PS2
  • Devil May Cry 4 - follows the story of a new devil hunter called Nero. It released in 2008 on the Xbox 360 and PS3 [It was given a Special Edition in 2015 providing more content to the game]
  • Devil May Cry HD: Collection - a HD remaster of the first 3 DMC games. It was released on the PS3 and Xbox 360 in 2012, and re-released on PS4, Xbox One and PC in 2018
  • Devil May Cry 5 - the latest installment in the series released in 2019 on PS4, Xbox One and PC. [It was given a Special Edition in 2020 for PS5 and Xbox Series X]
  • Devil May Cry: Pinnacle of Combat - a unique Chinese DMC experience to the mobile market [Currently in development by YunchangGame after being given permission by Capcom to create]

The order of release of the DMC games aren't in chronolgical order, to correct time-line based in the games are: 3 -> 1 -> 4 -> 2 -> 5
